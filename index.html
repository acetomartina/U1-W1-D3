Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow vour taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer toned with sweet caramelized onions. tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become vour new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cuDs (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive o1, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions. thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic winegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 1o ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    2. In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour.
